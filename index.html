Repairing a Technics SU-800 amplifier

Back in February, I started repairing an old Technics amplifier we had lying around. While it worked for a short while after being turned on, the sound quickly dropped out. After some digging around, I found the service manual and schematic for it, and started troubleshooting. I removed the amplifier from it’s casing, and reassembled it in a more debug-friendly way on my workbench (see picture). The amplifier, reassembled without enclosure.
